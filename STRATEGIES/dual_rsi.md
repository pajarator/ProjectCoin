# dual_rsi — Dual RSI (Long)

## Signal
Both RSI-14 and RSI-7 are oversold while short-term trend is still up.

## Entry Conditions
- RSI(14) < 40
- RSI(7) < 30
- SMA(9) > SMA(20) (uptrend confirmation)
- Guard: Price must be below SMA20 and Z < 0.5

## Exit Conditions
- **SL:** -0.3% from entry
- **SMA:** Price crosses above SMA20 (after 2 candles, in profit)
- **Z0:** Z-score reverts above +0.5 (after 2 candles, in profit)

## Timeframe
15m

## Coins
XLM

## Performance
79.3% avg win rate (RUN4.2). Requires both timeframe RSIs to agree on oversold.
